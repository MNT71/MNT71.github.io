Benchmark 14-56: Accepting positive criticism

In the Investors Club, we decided to revive the tradition of the CEO challenge at the career center. For this year's challenge, my group, Luke Molina, Nick Allen, Glen Starkey, and James Messer, developed the product "MooThane," a methane collection device from cows. This initiative directly aligns with Benchmark 14-56 beacuse after each presentation, the panel of judges provided feedback and posed various questions. We then utilized this constructive criticism to make effective changes to our presentation, ultimately leading to a refined and improved delivery. This demonstrates our willingness to accept and integrate feedback to enhance our performance, a crucial skill set for personal and professional growth. Embracing positive criticism not only fosters continual improvement but also cultivates resilience and adaptability, qualities that are highly valued in any engineering, where problem-solving and innovation are paramount.
