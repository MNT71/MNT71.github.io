Benchmark 17-78: Carrying out an assignment

Mr. Mullnix's AP Government class, my group comprised David Martin, Luke Molina, and myself. Our assignment was to develop a presentation addressing a social issue and conduct a poll among our peers. We chose immigration, polling 19 people, and utilizing different polling and graphing methods to share our data with the class. We asked various questions ranging from the importance of the border wall to the difficulty of becoming a U.S. citizen. This presentation not only addressed the social issue of immigration but also demonstrated proficiency in combining and utilizing various information sources with graphs and charts, aligning well with Benchmark 02-11's objectives. Proficiency in Benchmark 02-11 is essential for success in engineering careers as it enables engineers to analyze data effectively, understand technical documentation, visualize designs, collaborate across disciplines, and solve complex problems efficiently.
