Benchmark 18-80: Identifying different forms of creative expression

During my junior year in Mrs. Messr's AP Language and Composition class, we were tasked with selecting a book for personal reading and subsequently developing a presentation based on our chosen text. As an enthusiast of space exploration, I selected "Liftoff" by Eric Berger, a compelling narrative that chronicles the early days of SpaceX. For my presentation, I delved deeply into the book's content, crafting three distinct claims and substantiating each with specific quotes drawn directly from the text. This approach not only enriched my presentation but also served as a practical demonstration of Benchmark 18-80, which emphasizes the importance of engaging with literary works as a means to identify and appreciate various forms of creative expression. By choosing a book that intersected with my interests in aerospace, I highlighted the powerful connections between literature, personal passions, and broader interdisciplinary themes. This Benchmark is particularly beneficial for my aspirations in engineering, as it encouraged me to appreciate and incorporate diverse forms of creativity and expression.
